Selection criteria for ZEPETO Official Creators
  • 1

    Create content regularly on ZEPETO such as: Items, Worlds, Photos, Videos, etc.

  • 2

    50,000+ followers on ZEPETO or 1,000+ followers on social media: YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitch, Facebook and Twitter

  • 3

    At least one post made on ZEPETO or on social media within the last 30 days.

  • 4

    No history of being restricted or sanctioned for violating ZEPETO Community Guidelines within the past 6 months.

FAQ

What if I don’t meet the eligibility criteria?
Application may be rejected if the criteria for screening are not met when applying for the Program. Please review the checklist of screening criteria first before applying.
If I apply to become an Official Creator and meet the requirements, will I hear a response right away?
Please fill in the application form according to the instructions. Failure to follow the instructions may result in a rejection when we screen your application.
What are the major social media platforms that are evaluated during the selection period?
Major social media platform are YouTube, Instagram, Twitch, Facebook, and TikTok.
Can only individuals apply?
Anyone who wants to grow with ZEPETO, such as an individual or influencer, MCN, studios, and companies, can apply.
Is there an age limit?
At this time, children are not eligible to join the ZEPETO Official Creator Program. Applications received from minors may be rejected.
What happens if my follower count drops below the requirement after I apply?
Application may be rejected if the follower count is insufficient at the time of screening.
How will the review period and approval process proceed?
The screening process may take up to 30 days, and the screening may be delayed due to unforeseen circumstances.
Will I be informed if my application is rejected?
Only those who have been selected as Official Creators will be notified by email, and there will be no separate notices for those who are not selected.
If I get selected as on Official Creator, how long is the membership period?
The membership period to participate in the program is six (6) months, which shall be automatically renewed unless we receive a written request to leave the program 30 days in advance.
Can I withdraw my application or leave the program after I get approved?
You may withdraw your application or leave the program. After you submit a request to leave the program to the management team, we will guide you through the process. Please keep in mind that existing benefits will be removed and should you re-apply for participation in the program, acceptance is not guaranteed.
